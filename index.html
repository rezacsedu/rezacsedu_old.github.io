About myself

I am a Ph.D. Researcher at Insight Centre for Data Analytics, NUI Galway. I am working towards developing an abstract method for scientific knowledge discovery workflows with linked data to demonstrate its value by implementing with different big data sources in life sciences and biomedical domain throughout my Ph.D. studies. I have 8 years of experience in the area of research and software developments. Therefore, I have practical knowledge on software development process and life cycle with a strong working knowledge of algorithms and data structure. I have a Master of Engineering degree in Computer Engineering from the Kyung Hee University, Korea and a BS degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I worked with Samsung Electronics as a Lead Engineer for 3 years and have had the opportunity to work with distributed team members of Global Samsung R&D centers in India, Korea, Vietnam, and Turkey. Before that, I worked as a research assistant at the Kyung Hee University, Korea. As an R&D Engineer with BMTech21 Worldwide, Korea. And even before, I worked with i2SoftTechnology, Dhaka, Bangladesh as a Software Engineer.
